## **1. Introduction**  
### **1.1 Overview**  
The **CIBN Digital Library** is a comprehensive digital platform for the **Chartered Institute of Bankers of Nigeria (CIBN)**, offering:  
- **Digital Content**: Documents (PDFs), videos, audio files, e-books.  
- **Physical Resources**: Examination textbooks, stationery, souvenirs.  
- **Role-Based Access**:  
  - **Public Users**: Purchase general content.  
  - **CIBN Staff (Verified)**: Access exclusive materials (e.g., internal publications).  
  - **Admins**: Manage content, users, and orders.  

### **1.2 Goals**  
- Enable seamless access to **multimedia resources** (documents, videos, audio).  
- Integrate **Paystack** for secure payments (test credentials provided).  
- Ensure CIBN staff verification via **ID authentication**.

## **2. Target Users & Roles**  
| **Role**         | **Permissions** |

| **Subscriber**   | Purchase general content. |  
| **CIBN Member**   | Access exclusive content (verified via ID). |  
| **Admin**        | Full system control (content, users, orders). |

## **3. Key Features**  
### **3.1 Authentication & Access Control**  
#### **Login Flow**  
1. **User Selection**:  
   - **CIBN Member**: Enter **Employee ID** ‚Üí Backend verification ‚Üí Dashboard.  
   - **General User**: Email/password or social login ‚Üí Dashboard.  

#### **Exclusive Content Access**  
- CIBN Staff see a **"Restricted" tag** on exclusive items.  
- Non-staff users receive: *"Upgrade your account for access"* prompt.  

### **3.2 Content Types & Delivery**  
| **Type**       | **Format**          | **Access** |  
|----------------|---------------------|------------|  
| Documents      | PDF, Word           | Download   |  
| Videos         | MP4, Streaming      | Online     |  
| Audio          | MP3, Streaming      | Online     |  
| Physical Items | Books, Stationery   | Shipping   |  

### **3.3 Search & Filtering**  
- **Filters**:  
  - Content type (Document/Video/Audio/Physical).  
  - Category (Exam texts, CIBN publications, etc.).  
  - Price range.
